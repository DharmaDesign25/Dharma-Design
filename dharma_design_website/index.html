About Us

Dharma Design is Bhutan's first dedicated brass Buddhist artifacts manufacturing unit. Located in Ramtokto, Thimphu, we specialize in creating handcrafted statues and religious items using pure copper and traditional wax casting methods. Our work is certified by the Department of Trade and approved by the Department of Culture.
